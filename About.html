About Thrift Store Jhapa

Discover our story, mission, and the team behind our sustainable fashion movement

Our Story

Thrift Store Jhapa was founded in 2010 with a simple yet impactful mission: to offer high-quality, gently-used items at affordable prices, while promoting sustainability and community engagement.

What began as a small local shop has grown into a beloved community hub, where people come to both sell and buy pre-loved treasures. From vintage clothing to unique home décor, we’ve built a place where sustainability and style go hand in hand.

Our commitment to quality, affordability, and environmental responsibility has made us a trusted destination for conscious shoppers and sellers alike in Jhapa and beyond. By choosing to buy or sell with us, you're part of a movement toward a more sustainable and connected world.

Our Mission

Welcome to Thrift Store Jhapa: Sustainable Shopping with a Purpose

  • Promote Sustainability

    • We believe in sustainability and environmental responsibility, and this philosophy influences both our shopping process and the selection of products we offer.

  • Support Local Charities

    • Our mission is to support local communities, and we actively contribute to local charities and causes that make a difference.

  • Build Community

    • We are committed to fostering a strong, supportive community where every member contributes to and benefits from a collaborative environment.
